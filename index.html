About Me

I was born in Buffalo, New York on March 29th, 1996. My family then moved to Rochester, New York when I was two years old, and I lived there for the next 20 years of my life.

After I graduated from high school, I enrolled at Rochester Institute of Technology to get a degree in chemistry. While at RIT, I developed a love for rock climbing and hiking.
